ABOUT THE PROJECT

This project is a visual exploration of the relationship between alcohol, mental health, and physical health.

It's no secret that alcohol consumption can cause major health problems, but we often get a couple of glasses of wine or beer to celebrate parties, birthdays, graduations... In this project, the aim is to answer questions such as: Is there a strong relationship between alcohol consumption and life expectancy? What about the corrleation between alcohol consumption and mental healthiness? Doesn't it give a temporary positive impact on our mood?
How is it different among beer, wine, and spirits? Do any other factors affect life expectancy?
